Overview

Fallen Order takes place after the events of Revenge of the Sith, when all of the Jedi have been purged from the galaxy at the command of Emperor Palpatine. You play as Cal, a Jedi Padawan who manged to escape the Empire and is now living a quiet, solitary life as a scrapper, shut from the Force.
